Chinese Pinyin Input Method
Pinyin
汉字

Pinyin could be continuous without space, or separated by space for each word or character.

The algorithm will try to correct possible mis spellings and also try to split long Pinyin sequences.

For example:

maozedong he jiangjieshi -- 毛泽东和蒋介石

maozedon he jiangjieshi -- 毛泽东和蒋介石

maozedonghejiangjieshi -- 毛泽东和蒋介石
